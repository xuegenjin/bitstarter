It will be a free encyclopedia about Weiqi (also known as Go in Japan, Baduk in Korea). It will allow Weiqi fans upload and download game records, professional or amateur. It will allow Weiqi fans to view and comment on game records. It will allow Weiqi fans to add stories that took place throughout Weiqi's history.

We believe this site will bring the millions of Weiqi fans around the world a complete archive of game records, a place Weiqi fans can go to interact and share experience with.
